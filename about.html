Founder & Owner

Aftabun Nahar

Fashion Curator · Entrepreneur · Dreamer

"

Every saree I choose carries a story — of a weaver's hands, of a woman's grace, of a culture that deserves to be celebrated.

"প্রতিটি শাড়ি একজন নারীর সৌন্দর্য ও আত্মবিশ্বাসের প্রকাশ।"

Aftabun Nahar started SK Fashion from a simple belief — that every woman in Bangladesh deserves access to authentic, beautiful, and affordable fashion. What began as a passion shared on Facebook has grown into a trusted name, built on personal relationships, honest quality, and genuine care for every customer.

From sourcing the finest Banarasi silks to handpicking every Jamdani weave, Aftabun brings her personal touch to every single piece in the collection — ensuring that when you shop at SK Fashion, you're not just buying a saree, you're investing in something truly special.
